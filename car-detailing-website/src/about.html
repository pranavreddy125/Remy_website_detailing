About Desert Shine Detailing

We deliver meticulous car care — inside and out.

Our Mission

At Desert Shine Detailing, we strive to provide the highest-quality service to keep your vehicle looking its absolute best. Proudly serving the Phoenix area, our mission is to bring professional, reliable, and consistent care to every car we touch.

Our Background

Desert Shine Detailing was founded on a passion for clean, well-kept vehicles and a dedication to quality. Proudly serving the Phoenix area, our background is built on hands-on experience, attention to detail, and a commitment to treating every car with care.

Mobile Detailing Answers

We provide a full range of premium detailing services including deep interior cleaning, stain and odor removal, exterior washing, waxing, paint polishing, ceramic coating, and routine maintenance washes. Every service is designed to restore, protect, and enhance your vehicle’s appearance with professional-grade care.

You can schedule an appointment directly through our online booking page. Simply choose your service, select an available date and time, and provide your location. Once submitted, you’ll receive a confirmation and we’ll arrive fully prepared to handle your detailing needs.

Yes! We offer mobile detailing throughout most of the Wichita metro area. Just include your address during booking, and we will arrive equipped with all tools and supplies needed to service your vehicle on-site for maximum convenience.

We use high-quality, eco‑friendly detailing products from trusted brands such as Meguiar’s and Chemical Guys. Every product is carefully selected to be safe, effective, and capable of delivering long‑lasting results while protecting your vehicle’s surfaces.
